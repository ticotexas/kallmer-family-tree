# The Story Behind the Nickname

## A Misleading Stature

If we were to ask you how Floyd Kallmer the appliance man, acquired his nickname of "Stub" you'd probably never guess. It wasn't because of his somewhat diminutive stature, although that would be a logical conclusion.

## The Barefoot Kid from Odebolt

No, it was attached to him while he was a barefoot kid at Odebolt. Seems that Floyd and his boy cousin were playmates. Unfortunately Young Kallmer developed a propensity for stubbing his toe. So much so that this particular portion of his anatomy was almost constantly bandaged.

## The Real Story

His kid cousin started to call him "Stub" because of his wounded big toe. Others took it up. Result — he'll always bear the title, no doubt.

## From Buena Vista Onward

While he was playing football at Buena Vista, most of the fans never knew he had any other name than "Stub." But after he graduated he went down south with Firestone. Here he went by his real name. That is, until some of his friends started to writing him, addressing letters and cards to "Stub" Kallmer. So he couldn't get away, even down there.
