# The Salesman. The Neighbor. The Golfer.

Nobody in Storm Lake didn't know Stub. He earned his nickname early by always going barefoot; played quarterback, small but mighty, for Buena Vista College; and built Kallmer Sales Company into a familiar and trusted Storm Lake business.

For decades he sold and serviced refrigerators, washers, and dryers for local families. He lost Edith in 1957, later married Dorcas McPherrin, and after selling his store in 1967, kept working — still selling, still showing up.

In later years he drove campers to Alaska, wintered in Arizona, golfed, fished, and kept 427 Geneseo as the family landing place for whoever came home.
